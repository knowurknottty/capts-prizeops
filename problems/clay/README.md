# Clay Millennium Problems — CAPT Strategy
## (Long-range research, not first cash targets)

Six unsolved problems, $1M each:
1. Birch and Swinnerton-Dyer Conjecture
2. Hodge Conjecture
3. Navier–Stokes Existence and Smoothness
4. P vs NP
5. Riemann Hypothesis
6. Yang–Mills Existence and Mass Gap

## Clay Submission Rules (critical)

- **No direct submissions** — must publish in qualifying outlet
- **Two-year waiting period** after publication
- **General acceptance** in mathematical community
- Clay board makes final decision

Source: https://www.claymath.org/millennium-problems/rules/

## CAPT Strategy (not "solve Riemann" — map the territory)

For each problem:
1. **Equivalent formulations** — compile known equivalences
2. **Known barrier ledger** — what approaches have been proven impossible
3. **Partial results** — what special cases are solved
4. **Formal definitions** — full Lean formalization of the problem statement
5. **Computational experiments** — test special cases numerically
6. **Proof gap maps** — what is known vs what remains
